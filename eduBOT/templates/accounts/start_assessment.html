What is a Programming Assessment?

This assessment will evaluate your programming skills through a series of coding challenges. Based on your performance, we'll recommend courses that match your skill level.

Assessment Details
  • The assessment will present multiple programming problems to solve.
  • You'll have a limited time for each question.
  • You can only use one programming language throughout the assessment.
  • Once started, you must complete the assessment in one session.
  • Your results will remain private and will only be used for course recommendations.
